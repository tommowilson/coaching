What This Website is About

Most people who find their way here are already thoughtful, self-aware, and trying to live with more honesty and ease. But something in them feels tight or out of rhythm — like they’ve lost touch with a quieter intelligence inside.

My work helps people come home to themselves again. Not by adding more effort, but by helping you sense what’s already here — the body’s own way of finding balance, clarity, and ease.

Two ways to work with me

Coaching Conversations

For people wanting to live and love with more honesty, clarity, and presence. Our sessions are a place to pause and listen — to what’s actually true underneath the noise. You might come with questions about relationships, direction, or simply how to live in a way that feels more you.

Feldenkrais

Through gentle movement and awareness, we retrain your nervous system to move — and live — with more freedom. Whether you come with tension, imbalance, or just a wish to feel more at ease in your body, this work helps you rediscover a sense of flow and support from within.
