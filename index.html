Feeling Out of Sync in Your Body, Mind or Relationships?

Bring more ease to your body, clarity to your mind, and depth to your relationships.

What This Website is About

If you've found your way here, you're probably thoughtful, self-aware and wanting more alignment. Yet something feels out-of-sync. Maybe it's...

  • A sore back
  • A conflict that won't resolve
  • A quiet longing for something different.

I work with people like you to explore exactly where you are at and to support you in embracing new possibilities in life. We can address tension in your body, difficulties in your relationships, or simply the desire to feel more at ease and alive.

This website is a way to work with me directly, and also a place for you to explore my writing and video content. I am a prolific creator and you should be able to get a flavour of the way that I love to support people by reading and watching my content.

Two ways to work with me

Body and mind are connected. They are both gateways to access the truths within you. That's why I offer both body-work and conversational-work. Both of these methods are doorways that help us collaboratively find your next most empowering steps

Coaching Conversations
